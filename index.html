Handmade in L.A.

Using the techniques of the danish furniture designers of the 1950s and 60s, DRIFTWOOD has built a strong following in the Los Angeles community. With a healthy mix of custom and traditional designs, you will get the perfect piece for your home or office.

Vintage vibe with a modern approach that sets us apart from typical mass-produced furniture.

Without the overhead of a showroom, DRIFTWOOD is able to use the finest materials and craftmanship all within your budget. We pride ourselves on making sure that you get exactly what you're looking for, so we put a lot of effort into the early stages of planning to learn how best to serve your needs.

Affordable custom pieces that really define your space and show off your personality.
